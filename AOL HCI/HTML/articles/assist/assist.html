PT KERETA API INDONESIA ASSIST WHOOSH IN MAXIMIZING EID AL-FITR TRAVELERS

CONTRIBUTORS

26 Mar 2024

Because of the heavy influx of traffic due to Eid Al-Fitr travels, PT Kereta Api Indonesia, which operates the regular commuter trains, are set to relieve some load from the hugely popular Whoosh high-speed rail, which travels from Jakarta to Bandung.

The VP of Public Relations of KAI, Joni Martinus, has said that they will assist the Whoosh service to maximize the load to carry this Eid season, whether from Jakarta to Bandung or the opposite. He added also that the Whoosh and the regular commuter trains are complementary to each other; ready to assist whenever one is overloaded.

According to him, people traveling in the Eid Al-Fitr season that need a fast travel time to their destinations would more frequently pick high-speed rail transport above any other. As for people with no need of urgency, they can pick the regular commuter trains between the Jakarta-Bandung corridor so as to not overwhelm the high-demand high-speed rail.
