BRAGA ROAD CLOSURE SAID TO BRINGS DOWN POLLUTION LEVELS

CONTRIBUTORS

16 May 2024

Braga Street in Bandung since two weeks ago, every Saturday and Sunday, closes its way for motor vehicles for a pedestrian only road. This car-free policy is deemed to have a positive effect and said to be effective in combating against high levels of air pollution prevalent within the city.

Asep Kuswara, a high-level official within the city in transportation matters, has said that the local people is receptive regarding the policy. Regarding the issue of the pollution, he added that the city is located within a valley, as such the pollution within are unable to “go up” and out of the city. because of that, any pollution-fighting measures are welcome as long as it is effective.

Seeing the effectiveness and pleasantness of the car-free Braga Street, plans have been made to implement similar measures on other roads as well; to the same effect. One of these roads is Sukarno Street, another iconic street within the city.
