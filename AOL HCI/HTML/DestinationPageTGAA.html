The Great Asia Africa, located in Lembang, Bandung, Indonesia, is a popular tourist destination offering a unique cultural experience. It's a theme park designed to showcase the diverse cultures and landscapes of seven countries: Indonesia, Thailand, Korea, Japan, India, the Middle East, and Africa.
