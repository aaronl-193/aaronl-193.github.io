Sapulidi Resert Hotel

Sapulidi Resort in Bandung is a rustic-style resort located in the Lembang area, offering a variety of amenities including a spa, outdoor pool, and dining options. The resort is known for its spa facilities, which provide a range of treatments to help guests relax and rejuvenate. The outdoor pool offers a refreshing escape from the heat, while the dining options cater to a variety of tastes and preferences. Sapulidi Resort is a popular choice for travelers looking to explore Bandung or enjoy a peaceful retreat, with convenient access to local attractions and activities. The resort is owned by the same people who own Sapu Lidi restaurant & resort in Bandung, sharing a similar aesthetic that focuses on the natural environment.
