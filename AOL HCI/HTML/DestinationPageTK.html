Tebing Keraton is a natural tourist attraction located in Kampung Ciharegem Puncak, Ciburial Village, Bandung, West Java, Indonesia. It offers beautiful and amazing natural scenery, in the form of a vast expanse of pine forest and steep cliffs.

• Private vehicle: From Bandung, follow Jalan Setiabudi - Jalan Lembang. Tebing Keraton is located on the right side of the road, after passing Terminal Lembang.
• Public transportation: From Leuwipanjang Terminal, Bandung, take an angkot (public transportation) heading to Lembang. Get off at Terminal Lembang and continue with an ojek (motorcycle taxi) or angkot heading to Dago Atas.
• Online motorcycle taxi: You can use online motorcycle taxi applications such as Gojek or Grab to go to Tebing Keraton.

• Adult: Rp 11,000
• Children: Rp 5,000
• Motorcycle parking: Rp 5,000
• Car parking: Rp 10,000

Every day: 07.00 - 17.00 WIB

• Enjoy the natural scenery: Tebing Keraton offers beautiful and amazing natural scenery, in the form of a vast expanse of pine forest and steep cliffs.
• Take pictures: Tebing Keraton is an instagramable place, with many interesting photo spots.
• Camping: There is a camping area at Tebing Keraton that can be used for camping.
• Exploring the caves: There are several caves around Tebing Keraton that can be explored.

• Wear comfortable clothes and shoes for outdoor activities.
• Bring a hat and sunscreen to protect yourself from the sun.
• Bring enough drinking water.
• Come in the morning or afternoon to avoid the crowds.
