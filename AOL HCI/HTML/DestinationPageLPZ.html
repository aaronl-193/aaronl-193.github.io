Lembang Park & Zoo is a zoo and amusement park located in Lembang, West Bandung Regency, West Java. It is a popular tourist destination for families, offering a variety of animals to see and activities to enjoy.

• By car, public Private vehicle: From Bandung, follow Jalan Setiabudi - Jalan Lembang. Lembang Park & Zoo is located on the right side of the road, just before the entrance to Lembang.
• Public transportation: From Leuwipanjang Terminal, Bandung, take an angkot (public transportation) heading to Lembang. Get off at Lembang Terminal and continue with an ojek (motorcycle taxi) or angkot heading to Lembang Park & Zoo.
• Online motorcycle taxi: You can use online motorcycle taxi applications such as Gojek or Grab to go to Lembang Park & Zoo., Grab.

• Weekday: Rp 50,000
• Weekend: Rp 75,000

Every day: 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM WIB

• See the animals: Lembang Park & Zoo has a variety of animals, including lions, tigers, elephants, giraffes, monkeys, and more.
• Feed the animals: You can feed some of the animals at Lembang Park & Zoo, such as deer, rabbits, and sheep.
• Ride the rides: Lembang Park & Zoo has a variety of rides for all ages, including a Ferris wheel, a carousel, and a roller coaster.
• Play games: Lembang Park & Zoo has a variety of games for all ages, including arcade games, water games, and a playground.
• Watch the shows: Lembang Park & Zoo has a variety of shows throughout the day, including animal shows, magic shows, and cultural shows.

• Wear comfortable clothes and shoes suitable for outdoor activities.
• Bring a hat and sunscreen to protect yourself from the sun.
• Bring enough cash because not all places in Lembang Park & Zoo accept non-cash payments.
