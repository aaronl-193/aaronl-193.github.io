Alun-Alun Bandung is a public square located in the heart of Bandung, West Java. It is a popular gathering place for locals and tourists alike, offering a variety of activities to enjoy.

• Public transportation: Alun-Alun Bandung is easily accessible by public transportation. Several angkot (public transportation) routes pass by the alun-alun, and it is also within walking distance of several bus stops and train stations.
• Online motorcycle taxi: You can use online motorcycle taxi applications such as Gojek or Grab to go to Alun-Alun Bandung.

Free!

Every day: 24 hours

• Relax and people-watch: Alun-Alun Bandung is a great place to relax and people-watch. There are always plenty of people going about their daily lives, and it is a great way to soak up the local atmosphere.
• Take a walk or jog: Alun-Alun Bandung is a great place to take a walk or jog. The square is surrounded by a large park, which is a perfect place to get some exercise.
• Feed the pigeons: Alun-Alun Bandung is home to a large population of pigeons. You can buy a bag of birdseed from one of the vendors around the square and feed the pigeons.
• Visit the Masjid Agung Bandung: The Masjid Agung Bandung is a beautiful mosque located on the north side of Alun-Alun Bandung. It is a popular tourist destination and a great place to learn about Islam.

• Be aware of your surroundings and take care of your belongings. Alun-Alun Bandung is a busy place, and there are always people looking for an opportunity to steal.
• Bring a hat and sunscreen to protect yourself from the sun.
• Bring cash because not all vendors around the alun-alun accept non-cash payments.
• Come in the morning or afternoon to avoid the crowds.
