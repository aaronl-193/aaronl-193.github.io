Dago Dreampark is a nature park located in Lembang, West Bandung Regency, West Java. This park offers various rides and attractions for all ages, such as:
• Exciting rides: Adventure Zone, Sky Bike, ATV, Flying Fox, Paintball, and many more.
• Instagramable photo spots: Love Island, Santorini, Alun-alun Eropa, Rumah Hobbit, and many more.
• Complete facilities: Restaurants, cafes, souvenir shops, prayer rooms, toilets, and a large parking area.

• Private vehicle: From Bandung, follow Jalan Setiabudi - Jalan Lembang. Dago Dreampark is located on the left side of the road.
• Public transportation: From Leuwipanjang Terminal, Bandung, take an angkot (public transportation) heading to Lembang. Get off at Lembang Terminal and continue with an ojek (motorcycle taxi) or angkot heading to Dago Atas.
• Online motorcycle taxi: You can use online motorcycle taxi applications such as Gojek or Grab to go to Dago Dreampark.

• Weekday: Rp 25,000 • Weekend: Rp 30,000 • Children (under 2 years old): Free

Every day: 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM WIB

• Ride the rides: Adventure Zone, Sky Bike, ATV, Flying Fox, Paintball, and many more.
• Take pictures at instagramable spots: Love Island, Santorini, Alun-alun Eropa, Rumah Hobbit, and many more.
• Enjoy the beautiful natural atmosphere: Dago Dreampark is surrounded by a cool and green pine forest.
• Relax at a cafe or restaurant: Dago Dreampark has many cafes and restaurants serving a variety of food and drinks.

• Wear comfortable clothes and shoes suitable for outdoor activities.
• Bring a hat and sunscreen to protect yourself from the sun.
• Bring enough cash because not all places in Dago Dreampark accept non-cash payments.
• Come in the morning or afternoon to avoid the crowds.
• Obey the rules and keep the park clean.
