CHINESE LION DANCE PERFORMED AT BANDUNG STATION

CONTRIBUTORS

10 Feb 2024

Coinciding with the passing of the Chinese New Year, event managers at the Bandung Railway Station has staged multiple performative acts, including the ubiquitous Lion Dance (termed “Barongsai”) to entertain passengers on the station.

The famous lion dance incorporates numerous acrobatic techniques and fluid body movement to create the hypnotic adrenaline-filled dance.

Passengers of the station was said to be fixated by the lion dance troupe, and became a prime entertainment hot spot at the station.

After the dance, numerous passengers photographed with the lion as selfies, only after the lion itself got a red packet (“Angpao”) as a symbol to pacify it.

The troupe was a major success, with the majority of passengers enjoying and fixated on the fiery performance brought on by the dancers and their lion facade.
