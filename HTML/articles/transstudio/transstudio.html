DISCOUNT TICKETS FOR TRANS STUDIO BANDUNG

CONTRIBUTORS

23 Mar 2024

The famous indoor theme park of Trans Studio Bandung is opening its gates wide with their limited period discount of their enter ticket. With its strategic location in the middle of Bandung proper, its location would not be an issue of accessibility.

With the ticket price cutoff from Rp 300,000.00 to Rp 100,000.00, Trans Studio Bandung Will become a hot spot, attracting many theme park goers from miles away.

Inside, there are various attractions ranging from the most intense to ones that the whole family can enjoy. Even better, the discounted ticket will grant access to all of the attractions available, meaning that it contains immeasurable value.

All in all, the discounted price is a great entry way into the theme park, and to enjoy the attractions within.
