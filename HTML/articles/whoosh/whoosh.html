MINISTRY OF TRANSPORTATION PREDICTS 1.42 MILLION WHOOSH HIGH-SPEED RAIL USERS

CONTRIBUTORS

18 Mar 2024

The Ministry of Transportation predicts an increase of passengers using the Whoosh High-Speed Rail from Jakarta to Bandung, due to the Eid Al-Fitr Season. The number comes up to 1.42 million passengers, from a survey conducted by the Transportation Policy Agency (Badan Kebijakan Transportasi).

Risal Wasal, the Director General of Railways of the Ministry of Transportation postulates that while the number of passengers does not exceed the other modes of transportation, the authorities will keep watch against any things out of order.

He also said in a press conference yesterday (17/3/2024), that ramp checks have already been commenced into 66.7 percent to completion.

Aside from the checks, anticipatory contingencies are being planned and mapped out to watch for the regular and reverse flow.
