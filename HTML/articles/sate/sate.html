DEMONSTRATIONS AT GEDUNG SATE

CONTRIBUTORS

19 Mar 2024

Gedung Sate, a historical building known for its services as a museum and the seat of the West Javan Governor, became the place of protests conducted by the Bandung Chapter of the Islamic Student Association (Himpunan Mahasiswa Islam) on Tuesday (19/3/2024).

Protests are not uncommon to see around this area, given that the site is a seat of power, particularly of the provincial authorities.

The protest aim to highlight the perceived inability of the government in handling the welfare of her citizens. The Bandung Chapter said that the rising prices of rice became one of the indicators for the mishandling, particularly in 2024.
