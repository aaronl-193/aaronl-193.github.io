ROAD CLOSURE ON CIMAHI AREA

CONTRIBUTORS

24 Mar 2024

Two stretches of roads near the Cimahi (satellite city near Bandung) Alun-Alun Area are going to be converted into night markets and mercantile Ramadan bazaar from the 23rd of March though to the 9th of April.

The two stretches of roads that are going to be closed as a whole is the Ria Road from the Pasar Atas Baru direction, and Djulaeha Karmita Road, in front of the DPRD Cimahi main administrative building.

The chief of police of Cimahi, Kompol Donny Irawan, has said that the activity will commence from the 23rd of March to the 9th of April, and the two road mentioned before is going to be closed because it is already permitted.

The added permission to use these two stretches of roads is for the activity of the local population to not be disturbed heavily by the bazaars.
