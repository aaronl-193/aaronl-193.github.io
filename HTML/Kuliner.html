Soerabi Bandung

Imagine biting into a warm, fluffy pancake, but this is no ordinary pancake. This is Soerabi Bandung, a delectable Indonesian delicacy that will captivate your taste buds from the very first bite. Hailing from the vibrant city of Bandung, this treat is a perfect blend of tradition and innovation, making it a must-try for food enthusiasts.

Lotek Karedok

The magic of Lotek Karedok lies in its irresistibly rich and aromatic peanut sauce. This sauce is a harmonious blend of roasted peanuts, aromatic garlic, spicy chilies, tangy tamarind, fragrant kaffir lime leaves, and a touch of palm sugar, all pounded together in a mortar and pestle to create a smooth, creamy, and slightly chunky dressing. Each bite is a burst of freshness from the vegetables, perfectly complemented by the nutty, sweet, and mildly spicy sauce.

Mie Kocok Bandung

Picture this: a steaming bowl filled with a tangle of soft, chewy yellow noodles, bathed in a rich, savory broth that is the soul of the dish. The broth, simmered to perfection, is infused with the deep, hearty flavors of beef marrow, creating a luxurious, velvety texture that warms you from the inside out.

Sudirman Street

Sudirman Street in Bandung, also known as Sudirman Street Day & Night Market, is one of the city's most popular and bustling culinary destinations.

La Costilla

La Costilla in Bandung is a charming culinary spot that combines classic and modern interior design, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere perfect for dining with family, friends, or a significant other.

Tafso Barn

Tafso Barn in Bandung is a popular destination known for its unique combination of culinary delights and picturesque setting. This café offers breathtaking views of the surrounding hills and the city below, making it a favorite spot for both locals and tourists.

Lereng Anteng

Lereng Anteng Panoramic Coffee is a popular café that features a unique setup that blends with the natural terraced landscape of the hills, offering various seating options, including outdoor, semi-outdoor, and indoor areas.

Lawang Wangi

DLawangwangi Creative Space in Bandung is a captivating destination that merges art, nature, and culinary delights. This venue offers stunning panoramic views of Bandung, making it a perfect spot for both art enthusiasts and casual visitors.

Paskal Food Market

Paskal Food Market in Bandung is a vibrant culinary hub, teeming with a diverse array of food stalls and eateries that offer an eclectic mix of local and international cuisines.

Miss Bee Providore

Miss Bee Providore offers a unique dining experience that combines a cozy cafe setting with the concept of a modern marketplace. The venue features a warm and inviting interior adorned with rustic decor, creating a welcoming atmosphere for guests.
