Grafika Cikole is a nature resort located in Lembang, West Bandung Regency, West Java. It is a popular tourist destination for families, offering a variety of activities to enjoy, such as:
• Outdoor activities: ATV riding, paintball, flying fox, trekking, and outbound.
• Indoor activities: Camping, glamping, and karaoke.
• Family-friendly activities: Treetop adventure park, mini zoo, and playground.
• Culinary experiences: Several restaurants and cafes offering a variety of food and drinks.

• Private vehicle: From Bandung, follow Jalan Setiabudi - Jalan Lembang. Grafika Cikole is located on the right side of the road, approximately 18 kilometers from the city center.
• Public transportation: From Leuwipanjang Terminal, Bandung, take an angkot (public transportation) heading to Lembang. Get off at Lembang Terminal and continue with an ojek (motorcycle taxi) or angkot heading to Cikole.
• Online motorcycle taxi: You can use online motorcycle taxi applications such as Gojek or Grab to go to Grafika Cikole.

• Weekday: Rp 25,000
• Weekend: Rp 30,000

Every day: 24 hours.

• Explore the forest: Grafika Cikole is located in a beautiful pine forest, making it a great place to explore and enjoy the natural scenery.
• Try the outdoor activities: Grafika Cikole offers a variety of outdoor activities, such as ATV riding, paintball, flying fox, trekking, and outbound.
• Stay in a unique accommodation: Grafika Cikole offers a variety of unique accommodations, such as camping, glamping, and tree houses.

• Wear comfortable clothes and shoes suitable for outdoor activities.
• Bring a hat and sunscreen to protect yourself from the sun.
• Bring insect repellent to protect yourself from mosquitoes.
• Bring enough cash because not all places in Grafika Cikole accept non-cash payments.
